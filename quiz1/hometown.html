Where i came from?


Bekasi, a city located just east of Jakarta in Indonesia, is known for its rapid urban development and vibrant community. As one of the satellite cities of the capital, it offers a unique blend of modern amenities and traditional culture. The nightlights of Bekasi illuminate the city beautifully, with various shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment venues creating a lively atmosphere after sunset. Areas like Summarecon Bekasi and Mall Bekasi Cyber Park come alive with colorful lights and bustling activity, making it a great place for socializing, dining, and enjoying the vibrant nightlife. The well-lit streets enhance safety and create an inviting environment, showcasing the city's dynamic character as a hub for both residents and visitors.
