Adventure!?


Bekasi, a bustling city just outside Jakarta, offers a variety of attractions for both locals and tourists. One of the notable destinations is Danau BCBD Sumarecon, a beautiful lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for relaxing and enjoying nature. Visitors can engage in various activities such as jogging, cycling, or simply enjoying a picnic by the water. Another popular spot is GOR Bekasi, an indoor sports arena that hosts various events and competitions, from basketball to badminton. It serves as a hub for sports enthusiasts and offers facilities for training and community events. With its blend of natural beauty and sports facilities, Bekasi is an emerging destination for tourism, catering to a wide range of interests and activities.
